How to Reach IIT(ISM) Dhanbad

By Rail

Dhanbad Jn. (2.8 km away) is the largest station in the state with direct trains to almost every major city. Taxis, Autos, and Ola cabs are available 24/7.

By Road

Connected via NH-18 & NH-19. Seamless bus connectivity to Ranchi, Kolkata, Jamshedpur, and Bokaro.

By Air

Nearest Airports: * Durgapur (RDP): 85 km * Deoghar (DGH): 110 km * Ranchi (IXR): 140 km * Kolkata (CCU): 269 km

Places to See Nearby

Deoghar & Baidyanath Dham

Distance: 120 km (3 hrs)

Home to the revered Baba Baidyanath Jyotirlinga. Visit Trikut Pahar for panoramic views and the intricate Naulakha Temple.

Maithon Lake

Distance: 48 km (1 hr)

A serene reservoir on the Barakar River. Perfect for boating, fishing, and visiting the ancient Kalyaneshwari Temple and Deer Park.

Parasnath Hills

Distance: 50 km (45 mins)

Ajodhya Hills (Purulia)

Distance: 101 km (3 hrs)

A picturesque retreat with waterfalls like Bamni Falls, lush forests, and dams. Ideal for nature lovers.

Ranchi City

Distance: 150 km (4 hrs)

The city of waterfalls. Famous for Dassam Falls, Ranchi Lake, and the historic Jagannath Temple.
